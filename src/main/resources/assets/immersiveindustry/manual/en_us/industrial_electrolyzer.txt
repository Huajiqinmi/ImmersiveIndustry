Industrial Electrolyzer
Industrial Choice
<&industrial_electrolyzer>
It works faster, more efficiently, and can handle more Chemistry! Besides, it can collect gas heavier than air.
It requires <config;i;immersiveindustry.l_electrolyzerConsume>Flux/t constant input or the recipe would fail.
The redstone interface on the side will turn off the generator if supplied with a redstone signal.
You can also click at the electrode clamp at the top of the machine with electrodes to install electrodes.
