Steam Turbine
Not only steam engine!
<&steam_turbine>
Steam Turbine consume steam (64mb/t Max.) to produce power.
Steam is to be input at the pipe opening of the turbine, whereas energy will be output to up to two connections on the top. The <config;i;immersiveindustry.steamTurbineGenerator>Flux/t the generator outputs will be split between all connected points. The generator will only run when at least one output can accept energy.
The small terminal on the side will turn off the generator if supplied with a redstone signal.
